Micron, selling the high bandwidth memory shovel for the AI gold rush

1/29/2025

As this is my first blog post lets first catch you guys up on my current stock ideas. I currently hold Micron which I believe will stand to benefit in the next two years.

Micron sells DRAM/NAND and generally memory to other businesses similarly to other household names such as samsung and sk hynix. Now, to be honest this memory isn't anything

I care about too much because generally memory is considered an commodity as opposed to other semiconductor products. This is because basically memory is interchangeable from company to company.

Now why am I bringing all this bad news up when I think the company will do well? High bandwidth memory is different. Micron is currently dominating this field, outcompeting its competitors in Nvidia's latest chips. High bandwidth memory is used in

AMD and Nvidia's GPUs that are used to train AI. Some other things to note, with the coming tariffs on outside chips coming from the administration, micron positions itself as the largest HBM

manufacturer in the USA. They additionally also won a ton of money from the CHIPS act which will only increase their productions and hold on the HBM market. DeepSeek also just plunged the market

so the time is good to buy, as I believe it is trading at a discount. Its not all roses though, HBM is a competitive market, however Micron seems to have a comfortable lead and more tailwinds then most.
